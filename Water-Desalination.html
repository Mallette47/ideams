Container Greenhouse

The technology is not new, and uses forces in natures weather systems to produce rain. But what is new is that we can capture it inside of our system. The system is built in shipping container sizes so it can be shipped anyewhere in the world. 

Condensing Tower

The greenhouse container has sea water inside and as it gets heated up by the sun  the vapour raises up into the condensing tower. The condesing tower removes the heat and the water vapor condeses and collects in the water tank. The system not only desalinates the water but also raises the water without pump into the water tower.
